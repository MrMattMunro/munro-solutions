This policy explains how the game Floodline ("the game"), published by Munro Solutions Pty Ltd ("we", "us"), handles your information. By playing the game you agree to this policy.

We do not ask you to create an account or provide personal details, and we do not collect personal information directly. Your game progress (coins, best distance, unlocked vehicles, settings, and chosen character) is saved locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

The game uses the following third-party services, which may collect data under their own privacy policies:

Children

The game is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through a third-party service in the game, contact us and we will assist.

Data deletion

Because progress is stored only on your device, uninstalling the game removes that data. You can also reset advertising identifiers in your device settings.

We may update this policy as the game evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated effective date.
