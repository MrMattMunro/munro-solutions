Privacy Policy

Floodline — Munro Solutions Pty Ltd · Effective 25 June 2026

This policy explains how the game Floodline ("the game"), published by Munro Solutions Pty Ltd ("we", "us"), handles your information. By playing the game you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We do not collect any personal information. The game does not ask you to create an account or provide personal details. Your game progress (credits, best distance, unlocked vehicles, settings, and chosen character) is saved locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or to anyone else.

Advertising and analytics

The current version of Floodline displays no advertisements and contains no analytics, tracking, or third-party data-collection software. It does not access or use your device's advertising identifier.

In-app purchases

The current version of Floodline contains no in-app purchases.

Future updates

A future update may introduce optional extras — for example a rewarded advertisement you can choose to watch (via Google AdMob) and/or an optional one-off in-app purchase (via Google Play Billing). These are not present today. If they are ever added, this policy and the game's Google Play "Data safety" information will be updated to describe exactly what those services collect before the features are enabled.

Children

The game is intended for a general audience (ages 13+) and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. As noted above, the current version collects no personal information from anyone.

Data deletion

Because progress is stored only on your device, uninstalling the game removes that data. There is no server-side data to delete.

Changes

We may update this policy as the game evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with an updated effective date.
